python-register 
================

Linear and non-linear image registration methods, following the methods defined by:

| Lucas-Kanade 20 Years On: A Unifying Framework
| Simon Baker, Iain Matthews (2004)
| International Journal of Computer Vision 56 (3) p. 221-255

| Fast parametric elastic image registration.
| Jan Kybic, Michael Unser (2003)
| IEEE Transactions on Image Processing 12 (11) p. 1427-1442
